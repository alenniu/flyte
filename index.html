Frequently Asked Questions

Want Flyte but you have no idea how to get started? Go to any app store and download our app "Flyte"

Where can I download Flyte?

Flyte is available to all countries on IOS, Android and the Web. Simply go to any app store and search in Flyte, then download our free app to get started.

Do I need to give you my credit card information?

Flyte stores no payment information, however our restaurant partners need to get paid; so we use Stripe to process our payments and make sure your information is safe, and secure.

When I Sign-Up, my school isn't showing up to choose from?

Thats because Flyte has not arrived to your schoool yet, but no worries. Send us a quick email of your school name and our team will bring Flyte to your school as soon as possible.

I got a wrong order. What do I do?

Since the restaurants are humans and have to process many orders, mistakes happen from time to time. Just send us a quick email with your school, username, and recipt of the wrong order. We will refund your money back and you can enjoy a free meal.

How do I become a Flyte Driver?

Flyte is always looking for drivers. To ensure the safety of our clients, you must be a current driver of any major food delivery company and pass our quick identity check. Send us a quick email if you are interested.

I am a restaurant parter with Flyte but I haven't received any money for the orders.

All of our payments to each restaurant partner will be sent out by the end of each week. A full recipt of all orders made that week will be available on your tablet as well as emailed to you. Keep in mind that it may take 3-5 business days for the bank to send the money. If there are any miscalculations, feel free to contact us.
